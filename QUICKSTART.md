## Running the Application

The application is currently **running** at: **http://localhost:5173/**

Open your browser and navigate to this URL to start using FitTerminal!

## First Time Usage

### 1. Logging Food (Calorie Tracker)
Try these commands in the terminal input:
```
> 2 eggs, toast with butter
> chicken breast 200g
> quick lunch 600cal
```

### 2. Logging Activities (Cardio Tracker)
Quick add examples:
```
> 5k run 25min
> 30 min bike
> morning walk 45min
```

### 3. Logging Workouts (Fitness Logger)
Natural language workout input:
```
> Push day: bench 3x8 @185, ohp 4x6 @95
> Legs: squats 5x5 heavy, leg press 3x12
> 30 min cardio
```

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Tab**: Navigate between sections
- **Space**: Make current section fullscreen
- **Escape**: Exit fullscreen mode
- **Click sun/moon icon**: Toggle dark/light theme

## Features to Explore

1. **Macro Rings**: Visual representation of your protein, carbs, and fat intake (like Apple Activity Rings)
2. **Weekly Stats**: Track your cardio activities over the past week
3. **Personal Records**: The fitness logger tracks your PRs and celebrates new achievements
4. **Dark Mode**: Click the theme toggle for a terminal-green aesthetic

## Tips

- All data is saved automatically to your browser
- The food parser understands natural language
- Workout parser recognizes common exercise abbreviations
- You can click on any section header's maximize icon to go fullscreen
